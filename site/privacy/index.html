Local sessions and settings

Gyro stores sessions, workspace settings, logs, and local app state on your Mac. Provider credentials are stored through the macOS Keychain when that option is available. Gyro does not require a Gyro account.

What model providers receive

When you run a configured coding agent or model provider, that provider receives the prompts, selected files, tool context, and other content needed to complete your request. Its own privacy terms apply. Gyro does not operate a proxy service for that traffic.

Telemetry and this website

Gyro app telemetry is off by default. This website has no analytics, advertising cookies, account gate, remote fonts, or tracking pixels. Cloudflare serves the website, and GitHub serves release downloads. Both providers may process ordinary request metadata under their own policies.

Delete your data

Delete individual sessions in Gyro. To remove the app, move Gyro.app to the Trash. To remove local sessions and settings too, delete ~/Library/Application Support/Gyro. Data already sent to a configured provider must be handled through that provider.

Public alpha, license, and support
